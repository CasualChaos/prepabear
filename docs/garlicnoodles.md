## Spicy Garlic Noodles



Ingredients:
- Noodles (2 portions)
- Neutral Oil (¼ cup)
- Chili Flakes (2 tbsp)
- Soy Sauce (2 tbsp)
- Black Vinegar (1 tbsp)
- Sugar (1 tbsp)
- Garlic (4 cloves)
- Ginger (½ in knob)
- Green Onion (4 stalks)
- Sesame Seeds
- 500g Pork mince
